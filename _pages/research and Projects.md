## Research

<div class="jumbotron">
<div class="col-md-12 col-sm-12">
<h4>Spatio-temporal Analysis of Large Air Pollution Data</h4>
The work was to determine the zones and the time of air pollution occurrences from a large dataset containing data from different air pollution monitoring stations. My responsibility was to collect data, analyse it, and determine models for analysis as well as visualize the results. Time Series analysis and clustering were used to determine the pollutions hotspots and time of pollution.
